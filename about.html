About Me

Meet Dr. Catherine Leighton
With over 20 years experience, Dr Catherine Leighton's is an expert in helping organisations achieve their data goals by using their data to make better business decisions. Catherine's expertise in advanced analytical tools and techniques, together with her knowledge of people analytics, business analytics, and diversity analytics provides a rounded approach to complex business problems. Catherine enjoys the challenge of demystifying analytics and helping others understand and apply analytics to their workplace in an impactful way. Catherine's excellent communication skills means she is able to communicate complex analytical ideas and output in a meaningful and easily understood (non-technical) way.Further, Catherine has in-depth research skills which enable her to take an evidence-based approach to understanding business problems and developing data-driven solutions.

Catherine has extensive experience working with a variety of industry partners using data to help solve complex business problems, such as:
1) transport industry solutions (e.g. understanding attitudes towards driverless vehicles),
2) social impact complexities (e.g. intergenerational indigenous homelessness, values in not-for-profits, service integration),
3) negotiation behavior (e.g. the role of priming for good faith bargaining),
4) health workers attitudes and outcomes (e.g. nurse burnout and well-being), and
5) attitudes, emotions and work outcomes (e.g. managing emotions with co-workers and clients and well-being).
